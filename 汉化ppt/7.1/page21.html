Residual defect rates
7.1 Robustness & Correctness
▪ Residual defect rates refers to “bugs left over after the software
has shipped” per KLOC(千行代码):
– 1 - 10 defects/kloc: Typical industry software.
– 0.1 - 1 defects/kloc: High-quality validation. The Java libraries might
achieve this level of correctness.
– 0.01 - 0.1 defects/kloc: The very best, safety-critical validation. NASA
and companies like Praxis can achieve this level.
▪ This can be discouraging for large systems. For example, if you
have shipped a million lines of typical industry source code (1
defect/kloc), it means you missed 1000 bugs!

Software Validation
7.5 Testing and Test-First Programming
▪ Even with the best validation, it’s very hard to achieve perfect quality
in software.即使有最好的验证，也很难在软件中达到完美的质量。
▪ Here are some typical residual defect rates (bugs left over after the
software has shipped 残留缺陷率) per kloc (one thousand lines of
source code):
– 1 - 10 defects/kloc: Typical industry software(行业软件)
– 0.1 - 1 defects/kloc: High-quality validation 高质量验证的软件(E.g., the Java
libraries might achieve this level of correctness.)
– 0.01 - 0.1 defects/kloc: The very best, safety-critical validation. 安全攸关的软
件(E.g., NASA and companies like Praxis can achieve this level.)
▪ This can be discouraging for large systems. For example, if you have
shipped a million lines of typical industry source code (1 defect/kloc),
it means you missed 1000 bugs!

Refactoring at Google
9.1 Code Smells  Refactoring
▪ "At Google, refactoring is very important and necessary/inevitable
for any code base. If you're writing a new app quickly and adding
lots of features, your initial design will not be perfect. Ideally, do
small refactoring tasks early and often, as soon as there is a sign of a
problem."
▪ "Refactoring is unglamorous because it does not add features. At
many companies, people don't refactor because you don't get
promoted for it, and their code turns into hacky beasts."
▪ "Google feels refactoring is so important that there are company-
wide initiatives to make sure it is encouraged and rewarded."

Execution tracing
1.1 Multi-Dimensional Views of Software Construction
▪ Tracing involves a specialized use of logging to record information
about a program's execution.
▪ This information is typically used by programmers for debugging
purposes, and additionally, depending on the type and detail of
information contained in a trace log, by experienced system
administrators or technical-support personnel and by software monitoring
tools to diagnose common problems with software.

Debugger: breakpoint
7.4 Debugging
▪ In software development, a breakpoint is an intentional stopping
or pausing place in a program, put in place for debugging
purposes. It is also sometimes simply referred to as a pause.
▪ A breakpoint is like a tripwire(绊网) within a program: You set a
breakpoint at a particular “place” within your program, and when
execution reaches that point, the debugger will pause the
program’s execution, and then the programmer can examine the
current state of the program.
▪ 执行到断点处，调试器将暂停程序，以便观察程序的当前状态

9.2 Refactoring Techniques and Tool Support
Use Whitespace to Help the Reader
▪ Use consistent indentation.
▪ Put spaces within code lines to make them easy to read.
▪ Never use tab characters for indentation, only space characters.
– The reason for this rule is that different tools treat tab characters
differently — sometimes expanding them to 4 spaces, sometimes to 2
spaces, sometimes to 8.
– Always set your programming editor to insert space characters when you
press the Tab key.

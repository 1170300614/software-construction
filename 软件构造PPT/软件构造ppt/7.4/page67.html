Fix and check-in
7.4 Debugging
▪ Checking In 签入
– From the point of view of debugging, source control’s main value is as an
audit trail.
– If someone does introduce a regression, you should be able to find out
exactly which change did so (and therefore what you need to do to fix it)
by searching back through previous versions.
▪ The rule is one logical change, one check-in. 一次逻辑修改，只做一
次签入
▪ Make one change at a time

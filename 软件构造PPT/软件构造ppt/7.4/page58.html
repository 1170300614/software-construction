"Wolf fence" algorithm “狼栅栏”算法
7.4 Debugging
▪ Edward Gauss described this simple but very useful and now
famous algorithm in a 1982:
——"There's one wolf in Alaska; how do you find it?
——First build a fence down the middle of the state, wait for
the wolf to howl, determine which side of the fence it is on.
——Repeat process on that side only, until you get to the point
where you can see the wolf."

Diagnosis stratagem 1: Instrumentation
7.4 Debugging
▪ Instrumentation 插桩
– Instrumentation is code that doesn’t affect how the software behaves but
instead provides insight into why it behaves as it does. 插桩技术本身是代
码，其不影响软件本身的运行，帮助观察软件的运行。
– E.g., logging.
▪ Instrumentation isn’t limited to simple output statements,
however—you have the full facilities of the language at your
disposal.
▪ You can collect and collate data, evaluate arbitrary code, and test
for relevant conditions. 收集和整理数据，评估任意代码并测试相关
条件。

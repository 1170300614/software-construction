7.5 Testing and Test-First Programming
What to be tested in Integration Testing?
▪ Once all modules have been unit tested: “If they all work
individually, why do you doubt that they’ll work when we put them
together?” The problem, of course, is “putting them together”—
interfacing.
– Data can be lost across an interface; 数据丢失
– One component can have an inadvertent, adverse effect on another; 组件
相互影响
– Subfunctions, when combined, may not produce the desired major
function; 集成后不会得到期望的功能
– Individually acceptable imprecision may be magnified to unacceptable
levels; 单独执行中可接受的不精确可能会放大到不可接受的水平;
– Global data structures can present problems. 全局数据结构存在问题
– Sadly, the list goes on and on.
